Crimora coneja, common name the rabbit dorid, is a species of sea slug, a nudibranch, a shell-less marine gastropod mollusc in the family Polyceridae.


== Distribution ==
This species was described from California. It is reported from Point Loma, San Diego, California to Cape Arago, Oregon.
