Lethe anthedon, the northern pearly-eye, is a species of butterfly in the family Nymphalidae. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Lethe anthedon is 4568.1.For more images of Lethe (Enodia) anthedon, for more references, and for host plant information (grasses, and carex sedges) go to Enodia anthedon at Wikipedia. (Enodia = old genus name.)


== Subspecies ==

Lethe anthedon borealis (A. Clark, 1936)
