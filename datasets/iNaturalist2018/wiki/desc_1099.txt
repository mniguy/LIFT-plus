Staphylus mazans, the mazans scallopwing, is a species of spread-wing skipper in the butterfly family Hesperiidae. It is found in Central America and North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Staphylus mazans is 3931.


== Subspecies ==
These four subspecies belong to the species Staphylus mazans:

Staphylus mazans ascaphalus Staudinger, 1876
Staphylus mazans hayhurstii Edwards, 1870
Staphylus mazans mazans
Staphylus mazans tierra Evans, 1953
