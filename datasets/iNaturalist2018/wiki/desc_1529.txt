Myscelia cyananthe, the blackened bluewing, is a species of tropical brushfoot in the butterfly family Nymphalidae. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Myscelia cyananthe is 4531.


== Subspecies ==
These three subspecies belong to the species Myscelia cyananthe:

Myscelia cyananthe cyananthe
Myscelia cyananthe skinneri Mengel, 1894
Myscelia cyananthe streckeri Skinner, 1889
