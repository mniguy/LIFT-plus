Erynnis tristis, the mournful duskywing, is a species of spread-wing skipper in the butterfly family Hesperiidae. It is found in Central America, North America, and South America.The MONA or Hodges number for Erynnis tristis is 3953.


== Subspecies ==
These three subspecies belong to the species Erynnis tristis:

Erynnis tristis pattersoni Burns, 1964
Erynnis tristis tatius (W. H. Edwards, 1883)
Erynnis tristis tristis (Boisduval, 1852)
