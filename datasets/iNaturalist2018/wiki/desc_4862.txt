Lepidozona is a genus of chitons belonging to the family Ischnochitonidae.The species of this genus are found on the coasts of Pacific Ocean.


== Species ==
Species:
Lepidozona acostata Sirenko, 2016
Lepidozona allynsmithi Ferreira, 1974
Lepidozona amabilis (Berry, 1917)
