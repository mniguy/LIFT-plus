Panoquina panoquinoides, known generally as the obscure skipper or beach skipper, is a species of grass skipper in the butterfly family Hesperiidae. It is found in the Caribbean Sea, North America, and South America.The MONA or Hodges number for Panoquina panoquinoides is 4117.


== Subspecies ==
These four subspecies belong to the species Panoquina panoquinoides:

Panoquina panoquinoides calna Evans, 1955
Panoquina panoquinoides eugeon Godman & Salvin, 1896
Panoquina panoquinoides minima de Jong, 1983
Panoquina panoquinoides panoquinoides (Skinner, 1891)
