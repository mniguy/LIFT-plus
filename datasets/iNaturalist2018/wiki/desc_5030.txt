Clavulinopsis laeticolor is a coral mushroom in the family Clavariaceae. It has fruit bodies with slender, bright orange to yellow arms up to 5 cm (2 in) tall and 3 mm wide. It fruits singly or in loose groups on the ground, often among mosses. A widely distributed species, it is found in Asia, Europe, North America, and New Zealand.Similar species include Clavulinopsis fusiformis, Alloclavaria purpurea, Calocera cornea, Clavaria fragilis, and Macrotyphula juncea.
