Triphosa haesitata, the tissue moth, is a species of geometrid moth in the family Geometridae. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Triphosa haesitata is 7285.


== Subspecies ==
These two subspecies belong to the species Triphosa haesitata:

Triphosa haesitata affirmaria (Walker, 1861)
Triphosa haesitata haesitata (Guenée in Boisduval & Guenée, 1858)
