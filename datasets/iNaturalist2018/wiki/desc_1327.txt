Polia nimbosa, the stormy arches, is a species of cutworm or dart moth in the family Noctuidae. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Polia nimbosa is 10275.


== Subspecies ==
These three subspecies belong to the species Polia nimbosa:

Polia nimbosa mystica Smith, 1898
Polia nimbosa mysticoides Barnes & Benjamin, 1924
Polia nimbosa nimbosa
