Conopsis is a genus of snakes in the family Colubridae. The genus is endemic to Mexico.


== Species ==
Six species are recognized as being valid.
Conopsis acuta (Cope, 1886)
Conopsis amphisticha (H.M. Smith & Laufe, 1945)
Conopsis biserialis (Taylor & H.M. Smith, 1942)
Conopsis lineata (Kennicott, 1859)
Conopsis megalodon (Taylor & H.M. Smith, 1942)
Conopsis nasus Günther, 1858Nota bene: A binomial authority in parentheses indicates that the species was originally described in a genus other than Conopsis.
