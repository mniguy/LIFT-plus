Trachinops is a genus of ray-finned fish in the family Plesiopidae, the longfins or roundheads.


== Species ==
There are four recognised species in the genus:
Trachinops brauni Allen, 1977
Trachinops caudimaculatus McCoy, 1890
Trachinops noarlungae Glover, 1974
