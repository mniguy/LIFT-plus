Heliopyrgus domicella, or Erichson's white-skipper, is a species of spread-wing skipper in the butterfly family Hesperiidae. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Heliopyrgus domicella is 3970.


== Subspecies ==
These three subspecies belong to the species Heliopyrgus domicella:

Heliopyrgus domicella domicella (Erichson, 1849)
Heliopyrgus domicella margarita Bell, 1937
