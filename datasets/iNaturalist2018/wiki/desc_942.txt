Idaea demissaria, the red-bordered wave moth, is a species of geometrid moth in the family Geometridae. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Idaea demissaria is 7114.


== Subspecies ==
These three subspecies belong to the species Idaea demissaria:

Idaea demissaria columbia McDunnough, 1927
Idaea demissaria demissaria
Idaea demissaria ferrugata Packard, 1876
