Zopherus nodulosus is a species of ironclad beetle in the family Zopheridae. It is found in Central America and North America.


== Subspecies ==

These two subspecies belong to the species Zopherus nodulosus:

Zopherus nodulosus haldemani Horn, 1870 (Texas ironclad beetle)
Zopherus nodulosus nodulosus Solier, 1841
