Cercyonis oetus, the small wood-nymph or dark wood-nymph, is a butterfly of the family Nymphalidae. It is found in western North America.The wingspan is 32â€“45 mm. Adults are on wing from June to August in one generation.
The larvae feed on various grasses.


== Subspecies ==
There are four recognized subspecies:

Cercyonis oetus oetus
Cercyonis oetus charon (Edwards, 1872)
Cercyonis oetus silvestris (Edwards, 1861)
Cercyonis oetus pallescens T. & J. Emmel, 1971
