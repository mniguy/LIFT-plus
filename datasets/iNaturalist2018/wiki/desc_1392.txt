Schizura leptinoides, the black-blotched schizura or black-blotched prominent, is a species of moth in the family Notodontidae (the prominents). It was first described by Augustus Radcliffe Grote in 1864 and it is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Schizura leptinoides is 8011.
