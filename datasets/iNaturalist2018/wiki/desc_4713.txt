Maoricolpus roseus is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Turritellidae. A subspecies of this species has been described: Maoricolpus roseus manukauensis Powell, 1931. But it is now considered a synonym.


== Description ==
The length of the shell attains 41.8 mm.


== Distribution ==
This species found only in New Zealand.
