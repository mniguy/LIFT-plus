Eubaphe unicolor is a species of geometrid moth in the family Geometridae. It is found throughout North America to South America.The MONA or Hodges number for Eubaphe unicolor is 7444.


== Subspecies ==
These two subspecies belong to the species Eubaphe unicolor:

Eubaphe unicolor unicolor
Eubaphe unicolor venustata Fletcher, 1954
