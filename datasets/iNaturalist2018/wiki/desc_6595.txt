Pentachondra pumila, the carpet heath, is a small plant in the family Ericaceae.A semi alpine plant growing above the tree line in Australia and New Zealand. The form is in prostrate mats 3 cm high and 40 cm across. Frilly flowers and red fruit may appear at the same time.


== Distribution ==
In New Zealand this species is found in the North, South and Stewart Islands. Pentachondra pumila occurs in subalpine and alpine grassland, rocky and boggy places.
