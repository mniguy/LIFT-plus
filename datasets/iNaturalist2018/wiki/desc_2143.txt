Velarifictorus micado is a cricket in the subfamily Gryllinae (family Gryllidae) and tribe Modicogryllini.  A common name for Velarifictorus micado is the Japanese burrowing cricket.
The distribution range of Velarifictorus micado includes North America and Southern Asia.
