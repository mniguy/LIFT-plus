Diploblechnum fraseri, synonym Blechnum fraseri, known commonly as the miniature tree fern and by its MƒÅori name maukurangi, is a species of fern in the family Blechnaceae native to New Zealand, Malesia and Taiwan.


== Distribution ==
Diploblechnum fraseri is found in New Zealand, Malesia and Taiwan.


=== New Zealand ===
Its North Island range spans from Te Paki in Northland to near Mokau in Waikato. Its South Island range is limited to north-west Nelson.
