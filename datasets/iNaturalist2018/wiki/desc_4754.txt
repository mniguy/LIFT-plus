Corambe pacifica common name Pacific corambe is a species of sea slug, an Eastern Pacific Ocean nudibranch, a marine, opisthobranch gastropod mollusk in the family Corambidae.This species feeds on bryozoans.


== Distribution ==
Corambe pacifica are found on the west coast of North America.
