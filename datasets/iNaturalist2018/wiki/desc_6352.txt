Mammillaria grahamii is a species of cactus also known by the name Graham's nipple cactus.In the United States, this plant can be found in Arizona, California, New Mexico, and Texas.


== Description ==
This cactus has hooked stems and flowers pink or lavender in April and May. The cactus is commonly found in a dry habitat of gravel or grassland.
