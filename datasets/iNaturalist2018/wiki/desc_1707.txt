Emesis emesia, the curve-winged metalmark, is a species of metalmark in the butterfly family Riodinidae. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Emesis emesia is 4401.


== Subspecies ==
These two subspecies belong to the species Emesis emesia:

Emesis emesia emesia
Emesis emesia yucatanensis Godman & Salvin, 1886
