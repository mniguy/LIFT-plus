Dibaeis is a genus of lichenized fungi in the Icmadophilaceae family. The genus is widely distributed in tropical regions, and contains 13 species. Dibaeis was circumscribed in 1909 by Frederic Edward Clements; several species were transferred from other genera in a 1993 publication.
