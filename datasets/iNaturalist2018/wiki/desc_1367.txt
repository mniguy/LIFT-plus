Clostera apicalis, the apical prominent or red-marked tentmaker, is a species of moth in the family Notodontidae (the prominents). It was first described by Francis Walker in 1855 and it is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Clostera apicalis is 7901.


== Subspecies ==
Two subspecies belong to Clostera apicalis:

Clostera apicalis apicalis (Walker, 1855) i g
Clostera apicalis ornata (Grote and Robinson, 1868) i c gData sources: i = ITIS, c = Catalogue of Life, g = GBIF, b = BugGuide
