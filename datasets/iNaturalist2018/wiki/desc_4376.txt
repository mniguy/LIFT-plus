The large lizard genus Anolis contains around 436 accepted anole (US:  (listen)) species, which have been considered in a number of subgroups, or clades such as carolinensis and isolepis.

Nota bene: In the following list, a binomial authority in parentheses indicates that the species was originally described in a genus other than Anolis.
