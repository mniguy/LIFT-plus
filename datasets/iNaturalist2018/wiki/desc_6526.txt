Viburnum edule, the squashberry, mooseberry, moosewood viburnum, highbush cranberry, or lowbush cranberry is a species of shrub native to Canada and the northern parts of the US.The tart berries ripen early in spring and are eaten by various birds and mammals. With the seeds removed, they are edible to humans as well, and are made into jam.
