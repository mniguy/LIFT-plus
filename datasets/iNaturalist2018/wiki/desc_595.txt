Adela trigrapha is a moth of the  family Adelidae or fairy longhorn moths. It was described by Zeller in 1876. It is found in western North America, from Vancouver Island to California.

Females are smaller and have smaller eyes and antennae. They have a bright orange head. The forewings are bright metallic blue and the hindwings are purple.The larvae possibly feed on Leptosiphon androsaceus and Leptosiphon bicolor.
