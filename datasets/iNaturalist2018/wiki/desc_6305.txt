Gomphrena is a genus of plants in the family Amaranthaceae. They are known as the globe amaranths.
Species include:

Gomphrena agrestis
Gomphrena canescens
Gomphrena celosioides
Gomphrena globosa—Globe amaranth
Gomphrena haageana—Strawberry globe amaranth
Gomphrena perennis
Gomphrena pohlii
Gomphrena prostrata
Gomphrena pulchella
Gomphrena serrata
Gomphrena sonorae
