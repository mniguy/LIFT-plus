Anaea andria, known generally as the goatweed leafwing or goatweed butterfly, is a species of leafwing in the butterfly family Nymphalidae. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Anaea andria is 4554.


== Subspecies ==
These two subspecies belong to the species Anaea andria:

Anaea andria andria
Anaea andria andriaesta Johnson & Comstock, 1941
