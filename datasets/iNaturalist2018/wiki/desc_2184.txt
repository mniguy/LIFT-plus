Grapsus tenuicrustatus, commonly known as thin-shelled rock crab
or Natal lightfoot crab,
is a species of decapod crustacean in the family Grapsidae.


== Description ==
Carapace is a deep purple, almost black, with delicate lacy white markings all over its carapace and legs.


== Range ==
It is found in Hawaii and the Indo-Pacific.
