Lycia ypsilon, the woolly gray, is a species of geometrid moth in the family Geometridae. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Lycia ypsilon is 6652.


== Subspecies ==
These two subspecies belong to the species Lycia ypsilon:

Lycia ypsilon carlotta Hulst, 1896
Lycia ypsilon ypsilon
