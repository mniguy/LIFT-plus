Caria ino, the red-bordered metalmark, is a species of metalmark in the butterfly family Riodinidae. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Caria ino is 4396.


== Subspecies ==
These two subspecies belong to the species Caria ino:

Caria ino ino
Caria ino melicerta Schaus, 1890
