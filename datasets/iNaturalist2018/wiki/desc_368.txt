Pachypsylla is a genus of psyllids. Each of its four species lay eggs on the leaves of the Celtis occidentalis tree. Upon hatching, the young psyllids become encased in a "gall" which the young leaf parts grow in response to the infestation.


== Species ==
Species of Pachypsylla include:

Pachypsylla celtidisgemma – hackberry bud gall maker
Pachypsylla celtidismamma – hackberry nipplegall maker
Pachypsylla celtidisvesiculum – hackberry blistergall psyllid
Pachypsylla venusta – petiolegall psyllid
