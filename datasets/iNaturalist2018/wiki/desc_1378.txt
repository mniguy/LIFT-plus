Lochmaeus bilineata, the double-lined prominent moth,  is a moth of the  family Notodontidae. It is found in the eastern two-thirds of the United States and southern Canada, west to New Mexico in the south and Saskatchewan in the north.The wingspan is 32â€“40 mm. Adults have grey to brownish-grey forewings. They are on wing from April to October in the south and from June to August in the north.
