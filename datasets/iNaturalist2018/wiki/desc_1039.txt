Cabares potrillo, the potrillo skipper, is a species of dicot skipper in the butterfly family Hesperiidae. It is found in the Caribbean Sea, Central America, North America, and South America.The MONA or Hodges number for Cabares potrillo is 3916.


== Subspecies ==
These two subspecies belong to the species Cabares potrillo:

Cabares potrillo potrillo (Lucas, 1857)
Cabares potrillo reducta Mabille & Boullet, 1919
