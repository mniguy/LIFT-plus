Marathyssa inficita, the dark marathyssa, is a moth in the family Euteliidae. The species was first described by Francis Walker in 1865. It is found in North America.The MONA or Hodges number for Marathyssa inficita is 8955.


== Subspecies ==
It has two subspecies:

Marathyssa inficita inficita
Marathyssa inficita minus Dyar, 1921
